About

Mildly chaotic and manic in nature, unspecified and unclassified music-obsessed tech-loving hobbyist that moves against the flow with hope to reach something completely new and appreciated. Love to mess with mathematics of different sorts and kinds, linux-es (Mostly Debian and Gentoo, but occasionally won't mind messing with Alpine, Arch or Ubuntu), hardware, software and almost everything related. Writing code for fun and occasionally - for profit, most proficient in Rust and C++, but sometimes won't mind to write some C/Python/Shell. Believing that one day I'll find my place as game developer and trying to move in this direction, keeping the pace. Also trying to keep up with ever-evolving and ever-changing tech trends. Concerned about online privacy and data security in general. Interesed in eradication of DRM in favor of free data flow and believe this will be beneficial for both producers and consumers, thus drastically reducing need in piracy.

Tech aside I appreciate and enjoy good sci-fi/horror/animated movies, japanese animation, animated shows (Like MLP:FIM, Steven Universe, Gravity Falls, Infinity Train and a lot more). Listen to music most of the time, mainly electronic, rock and it's descendants, though my library is pretty diverse. From time to time I try myself in visual art, but this does not seem to be my thing. Occasionally love to dip into deep philosophical, metaphysical, psycological thoughts.

Able to communicate in english (Slightly above Advanced) and russian (Native), but prefer english as it is lingua-franca of the world.
